02 / Capabilities

What We Build.

Four Core Services

— 01

Gearbox Spares to OEM Standard

Drop-in replacement gears, shafts and sub-assemblies engineered to match original OEM specification — dimensional, metallurgical and functional.

— 02

Gearbox 1:1 Replacement

Form, fit and function equivalent gearboxes — reverse-engineered to dimensional envelopes and torque ratings, without re-piping or re-aligning.

— 03

Gearbox Refurbishment

End-of-life drives restored to new condition — strip, NDT inspection, re-gearing, re-bearing and load-test — at a fraction of new-unit cost and lead time.

— 04

Preventive Maintenance Suite

AI-enabled condition monitoring and scheduled service — vibration, thermal and wear-pattern analysis that flags failure modes before they interrupt production.
